Feature Richness

Upload Contacts

With Twilio's Sync Maps and Sync Lists, you can effortlessly upload and securely store a whopping 10,000 contact records.

Text Messaging

With Twilio's Programmable Messaging and Conversations, you can now easily send notifications and engage in two-way conversations with your clients without leaving the CRM interface.

Voice Calling

With our CRM for Twilio, you can make or receive crystal-clear voice calls right from your browser, thanks to Twilio's Programmable Voice technology.

Voicemails

Never miss a message from your contacts again! With automatic voicemail setup, your customers can easily leave you a message, ensuring you stay up-to-date with all their needs and concerns.

Histories

Easily track your communications in one central location with the ability to view both call logs and message logs. Never lose track of important conversations again!

More

Secured Architecture

  • Data is kept only in your browser or your Twilio account.
  • No middle man, we have no servers for processing your data.
  • Your information is never visible to anyone at SuperWorkerAI.
  • Your data is limited to you and anyone with access to your Twilio account.
