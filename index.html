CRM For Twilio

A Chrome Browser extension for Twilio
A Google Chrome browser extension that allows you to store contacts and communicate with your contacts over all of the support Channel APIs offered by Twilio.
Go To Extension Page

Feature Richness

Upload Contacts

Using Twilio's Sync Maps and Sync Lists, you can upload and keep a record of up to 10,000 contact records.

Text Messaging

Send notifications or have 2way conversations with your customers right from the CRM using Twilio's Programmable Messaging and Conversations.

Voice Calling

Make or receive voice calls right to your browser using Twilio's Programmable Voice..

Voicemails

Automatic setup to allow your contacts to leave you voicemail.

Histories

View your call logs and message logs right in one place.

Secured Architecture

  • Data is kept only in your browser or your Twilio account.
  • No middle man, we have no servers for processing your data.
  • Your information is never visible to anyone at SuperWorker.
  • Your data is limited to you and anyone with access to your Twilio account.
